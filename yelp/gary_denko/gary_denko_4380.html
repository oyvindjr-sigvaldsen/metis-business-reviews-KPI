The chocolate souffle served with a dark and white chocolate sauce was fantastic. A plate of small desserts followed, but which time we were completely full. They give a beautifully wrapped dessert to take home for the lady. In summary, this is a wonderful restaurant that I will visit next time I am in San Francisco, but the overall experience can not outshine my favorites such as Daniel in New York.
