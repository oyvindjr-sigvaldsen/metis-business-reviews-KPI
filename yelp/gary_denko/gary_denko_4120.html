They prepare it medium rare, and the only thing is that for me it was a little bit on the rare side --- if I had to do it again I would ask for medium. 4) Cheese Plate Our server gave us an overview of all the cheeses (about 20 in all), of which we were allowed to select 4. The cheeses included a wide variety, domestic, european, goat, blue, and the server was quite good in helping us choose. I particularly liked a cheese from Belgium called Chimay Grand Cru. Each dish was like a different meal in itself, each with myriad flavors, textures, presentations, yet the underlying thread throughout was how tasty every dish was.
