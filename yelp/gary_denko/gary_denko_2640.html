At the end of our meal, after we were all done, they gave us a complimentary pumpkin breakfast cake for the next day. Like I said, the attention to details and the wonderful service and the little add-ons here and there really set them apart from other restaurants. Our server was so wonderful and so attentive the whole time, without being over bearing. I really felt special in this restaurant. Next time, if I ever get the chance to go back, I'll probably just do the 3 course. The meal took about 2 hours and it wasn't just a meal, but a full on dining experience. Wonderful!!!!
