Found out about this place only because my partner surprised me with an anniversary dinner here. We were lucky to get last-minute reservations as they are booked 2 months out. Even though we got there 20 minutes early for a 9:30 reservation the place was packed and people were routinely waiting 30 minutes to be seated. The staff were warm and friendly and apologized for the wait. The food was great, as all the reviews have said. Here is a critique of our dinner. We started with the crab salad and lobster risotto. The salad was fresh and meyer lemon vinaigrette was just right. The risotto was good, just slightly salty for my taste. We read mixed reviews on the scallops but loved them. The branzini sea bass skin was nice and crispy but the meat was dry. I was daring and tried the bison. Very good, not gamy with a good sauce. We ended with the chocolate souffle'. Not too sweet. A little more chocolate syrup would've been perfect. Don't forget to ask the waiter to pour you more if you have a sweet tooth! The wait-staff were very professionaly. Trained well. They do not smother you, but are quick to respond to any requests you may have. In the end, they brought out a nice extra dessert with a candle and Happy Anniversary written in chocolate on the plate. An unexpected nice touch.
