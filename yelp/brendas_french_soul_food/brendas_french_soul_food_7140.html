    Whatever goes through the mind of an idiot like this before he introduces a slimy smurf to the sidewalk, it must be completely thoughtless and brain-dead.  Maybe "Fuck you universe for putting me here, so here, try turning THIS into a planet!"   That also caused three stars to go supernova, which is why you see two right now, as you read this.  Oh, and here are the answers to the questions stirring in yer head.  Yes, that cook DID work at Brenda's, as I've seen him working there at least 4 or 5 times before.  Yup, I do judge a whole restaurant by smoking cooks who SPLORRFFF slippery Brendaballs right outside for customers to track right back into the restaurant on their shoes.  And ch'yeah, I can write this if I want to.  Why yes, those are MY stars and I can do what I want with them.  And um yeah, this was back in 2012, but it doesn't matter.  That image still plagues my impression of Brenda's Soul Food even to this day.

    And YES, I did try to address this to management.  I called the Brenda-line later on that night, and I kept getting a busy signal.  I finally got through to the front desk, and although it was noisy, I asked if Brenda was available.  She was not, so I attempted to explain to the host what happened that night.  She acted and responded as though she didn't understand what I was saying, or she was just playing stupid.  I called the next day, and the lunch host offered to leave a message for Brenda, so I did.  And..............................................  (silence)

    I can't imagine anything that this restaurant could do to rewind, erase, or otherwise lobotomize me to forget that this ever happened.  Unfortunately I don't know the name of that cook either, so we shall never know whether he was eventually fired for other misdeeds.
