First, I'd start with a simple meringue to warm up the palate - terrifically whipped egg whites and pleasant amounts of sugar with a satisfying crunch, and no flour filler. Then we'd move onto the two-bite Friands, valrhona chocolate tarts topped with ganache - rich and delicately bitter. Next up, breakfast! The Frangipane croissant, flaky and buttery studded with shaved almonds outside and almond paste in, I wish I had a lifetime supply of these. And for dessert, my current sweet obsession: banana cream pie. But no need to end on a bitter note, the vitrine is brimming with delights that are well worth a gander and munch. For best results, skip the interminable weekend queue by playing hooky or popping over for lunch, then snag some goodies for a Dolores Park picnic!
