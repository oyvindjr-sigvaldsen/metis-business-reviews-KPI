    The lemon was yummy also, with a slight tang and very creamy. There was a petal on top and the crust was harder than the banana cream tart, and just as good.

    Now here's the disappointing part: I didn't like the bread pudding at all. It was bland and didn't taste like anything, except, well bread. My friend also recommended this to me. That's strike one, buddy.

    Next time I visit, I'm going to try the regular items and more pastries.
